PHASE 1Now — ~40 € Ready to buy

✓
Real analog 5.1 surround from the gaming PC + HA control of both receivers — for just 40 €. No new amplifier needed.

Office — Gaming Surround

  1. Buy 3× jack-to-RCA cables (~15 €)
  2. B650: green→FL/FR, black→SL/SR, orange→C/Sub on the STR-DB790 "Multi Ch In"
  3. Windows: Playback devices → "Speakers" → Properties → Surround 5.1
  4. STR-DB790: switch input to "Multi Ch" / "6ch Direct"

Living Room — Movie Surround

  1. Optical TOSLINK cable TV → STR-DB790 living room (~5 €)
  2. TV: set audio output to "Optical / Dolby Digital"
  3. STR-DB790: switch input to "Optical"
  4. Done — Dolby Digital 5.1 directly from the TV

📡

⏳
Only buy once Phase 1 (OptiPlex + rack) is running and the PowerEdge is funded. The STR-DB790 units will then be used as pure power amps — not thrown away.
💡
Why not Yamaha? Yamaha RX-V and RX-A (mid-range) only have a subwoofer pre-out — all other channels have no pre-amp output. The STR-DB790 can't be used as a power amp with those. Only the Denon X3300W/X3400H or Marantz SR5010+ have full 7.2 pre-outs.
