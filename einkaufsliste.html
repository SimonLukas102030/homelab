5 · DIY Custom Panel 1U — USB + HDMI + 4×1 Switch ~75–90 €

The 1U panel is the central patch point between rack and desk. Front face (left to right): [USB-A] [USB-A] [USB-C] │ [HDMI IN quick] │ [1] [2] [3] [4] HDMI select │ Cable reels → USB section: USB hub inside panel → 2× USB-A + 1× USB-C on front. Powers and passes data. Cable reels (Command Wall) connect here.
HDMI 4×1 switch section: Gaming PC uses DisplayPort → G9 directly (main connection). The G9's single remaining HDMI port gets fed from this switch's output. 4 inputs:
— Input 1: Front-panel HDMI socket (quick connect anything at rack front)
— Input 2: Rear pigtail → OptiPlex HDMI
— Input 3: Rear pigtail → Raspberry Pi mini-HDMI
— Input 4: Rear pigtail → spare (console, laptop)
Output: Rear pigtail → long HDMI cable (~2.5m) to G9
Selection buttons: Most 4×1 HDMI switches have 4 individual buttons on the body. Remove the enclosure, install PCB inside 1U housing, align the 4 buttons with holes drilled in the panel face. LEDs per port show active input. Simon builds this himself — see below for parts.
5a — 1U panel blank (Adam Hall 87221STL): thomann.de ~€4
5b — HDMI 4×1 switch (4 individual buttons, IR remote): Amazon ~€25 — search "HDMI Switch 4x1 4K 60Hz individual button" — look for units where the 4 buttons are on the top/side face so they can be exposed through panel cutouts. Examples: Avedio links, NÖRDIC, or generic 4K 60Hz 4×1 box
5c — 5× HDMI panel-mount female connectors (bulkhead): Amazon ~€12 — 1 for front input, 4 for rear (3 inputs + 1 output pigtail adapter)

Buy this NOW, before the height-adjustable desk frame (Phase 3). PHASE 3Minijob Upgrades — independent of Phase 1/2

💡
Phase 3 is not tied to Phase 1 or 2 timing — buy these as Minijob money accumulates. Priority order: chair first (daily impact), then printer, then desk frame, then monitor arm. All desk items are planned for the 160 cm × 80 cm × 1.5 cm thick tabletop.

A · Ergonomic Office Chair — full mesh, not gaming ~230–400 €

Most important Phase 3 purchase — you'll spend thousands of hours in it. Key requirements: full mesh back (breathable, doesn't overheat), adjustable lumbar support, 4D armrests, seat depth adjustment, strong tilt tension. No gaming chair designs — they have wrong ergonomics for desk work (reclined spine, wrong lumbar position). Must feel comfortable after 6+ hours.
A1 — Budget: Sihoo M57 (~€230–260): Full mesh, 3-way armrests, adjustable lumbar, headrest, good build quality for the price. Available on sihoooffice.de and Amazon.de
A2 — Mid: Hbada E3 Pro 2026 (~€300–350 without footrest): 3-zone adaptive lumbar, 4D armrests, CloudMesh material, very good adjustability. hbada.eu
A3 — Best value used (~€400–600): Steelcase Leap V2 or HM Mirra 2 from Kleinanzeigen — office furniture liquidations often have these. Built to last 20+ years, absolute best ergonomics. Worth searching before buying new.

B · Printer / Scanner — Brother laser ~178–200 €

Brother DCP-L2620DW: monochrome laser, 32 ppm, ADF scanner (automatic document feeder), duplex, WiFi + USB. Laser = toner lasts ~1,200 pages per cartridge, no drying out, no expensive ink subscriptions. No HP (ink costs are brutal). Goes on TOP of the open frame rack (set flat on the top rail crossbar, 6.3 kg, accessible at ~145 cm height).
Dimensions: 360 × 360 × 270 mm — does NOT fit inside a 1U or 2U slot; placing on rack top is the correct approach.

C · Height-Adjustable Desk Frame (Tischgestell) ~200–280 €

For the 160 cm × 80 cm × 1.5 cm thick tabletop. The frame mounts under the existing top — no new tabletop needed. Height range: typically 58–123 cm (sitting to standing). Memory presets (4 positions recommended — sitting low, sitting standard, standing, standing tall). Dual motor (3-stage telescoping legs) is significantly more stable than single motor for a 160 cm span at full height.
1.5 cm tabletop thickness: All major brands support this — the mounting brackets clamp from underneath, no minimum thickness requirement beyond ~12 mm.
Desk cable management (item 8) must be installed first — the cable spine is designed for the desk's height travel range from day one.
C1 — Budget: FlexiSpot EG1 (~€200): Single motor, 71–121 cm range, 70 kg capacity, 4 memory presets. Width adjustable up to 160 cm. Amazon.de
C2 — Recommended: FlexiSpot E5 (~€280): Geizhals from €280 — dual motor, 58–123 cm range, 125 kg capacity, 3-stage telescoping, much more stable at max height (important for 160 cm span). Confirmed in stock.
flexispot.de direct: flexispot.de E5 — sometimes on sale up to €30 off
