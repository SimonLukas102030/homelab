PANELDIY Custom Panel — U3

1U blank panel is the central interface between rack internals and the desk. Built by hand — the HDMI switch PCB is stripped from its housing, mounted inside the 1U panel, and its 4 selection buttons exposed through cutouts in the panel face.

Front face layout

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐ │ [USB-A] [USB-A] [USB-C] │ [HDMI ▶] │ [1] [2] [3] [4] │ ≈≈≈≈≈ │ └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘ ← USB hub ← HDMI IN ← select ← cable reels
USB section
Internal USB hub → 2× USB-A + 1× USB-C on front. Charges and passes data to PC. Cable reels (Command Wall) connect here.
HDMI IN socket
Front-panel HDMI female (Input 1 of switch) — quick connect for laptop, console, camera.
Select buttons [1][2][3][4]
HDMI switch PCB mounted inside, 4 buttons drilled through panel face. LED per port shows active input.
Cable reels slot
Fasgear retractable USB-C reels pass through slots to Command Wall exterior.
